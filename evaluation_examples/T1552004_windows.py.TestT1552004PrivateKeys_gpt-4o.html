1
2
3
     find / -name "*.key" 2>/dev/null

- **Windows**: Use PowerShell. 
1
2
     Get-ChildItem -Path C:\ -Include *.key,*.pgp,*.gpg,*.ppk,*.p12,*.pem,*.pfx,*.cer,*.p7b,*.asc -Recurse -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
