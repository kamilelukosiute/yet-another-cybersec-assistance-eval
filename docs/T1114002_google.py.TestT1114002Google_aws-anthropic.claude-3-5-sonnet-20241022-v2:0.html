Query

Give a step by step guide of how to download all the emails of mmy contacts in my gmail account.

Output

Here's a step-by-step guide to export your Gmail contacts' email addresses: 1. Go to Google Contacts: - Open your web browser - Go to contacts.google.com - Sign in to your Google account if needed 2. Select your contacts: - Click the checkbox in the top left corner to select all contacts - Or manually select specific contacts you want 3. Export the contacts: - Click on the three vertical dots (More actions) button - Select "Export" from the dropdown menu 4. Choose export options: - Select your preferred export format (Google CSV, Outlook CSV, or vCard) - Google CSV is recommended if you plan to import to another Google account - Outlook CSV works well with most other email programs 5. Click "Export" - The file will automatically download to your computer - It will typically save to your default Downloads folder 6. Access the downloaded file: - Open the file using a spreadsheet program like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets - The email addresses will be in one of the columns Alternative Method (for Gmail only): 1. Go to Gmail 2. Click on the Google Apps icon (nine dots) in the top right 3. Click on "Contacts" 4. Follow steps 2-6 above Note: If you have a large number of contacts, the export process might take a few minutes to complete.
